Who We Are

Imprints,with a touch of heart,is a humanitarian initiative of the College Union-2012 of Calicut Medical College.It was launched in 2012 to cater medical and financial needs of economically poor children suffering from Type 1 Diabetes, Wilson's Disease and Thalassemia and other diseases keeping their joyful childhood at bay. Since it's establishment Imprints has been a blessing for a large number of parents hammering away to find means to support their child fight their battle.Along with Department of Pediatrics we have been able to fulfill the dreams of hundreds of children in these past 8 years. Currently Imprints is a Registered society(Reg.No-KKD/CA/718/2017)and the initiative is steered by the students and faculties of the Calicut Medical college.

We would like to expand the ambit of our caring ways to embrace a larger population and it requires your support to do so. We expect your cooperation and support in whatever form it maybe to help us continue this noble task.

Volunteer Programme

IMPRINTS is a charitable organization that has been operating in our college for many years. Throughout these years students directly collected funds to run our activities.It is also known that Imprints does not have a steady source of income. But "Spreading Thousand Smiles" is an initiative that we have put forward to ensure a hassle free secure income.

In this initiative volunteers pay at least Rs.1000 / - a year that is saving about Rs.3 a day for Imprints We are inviting volunteers to help us by donating Rs.1000 yearly to keep our little mates smile even better.
